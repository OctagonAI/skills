# Earnings Revenue Guidance Skill

## Purpose
Extract specific revenue guidance and growth projections with segment and currency breakdown.

## When to Use
- Detailed revenue projections
- Segment revenue breakdown
- FX impact analysis
- M&A contribution separation

## Example Prompt
```
Extract specific revenue guidance and growth projections from CRM's latest earnings call transcript.
```

## Key Information Returned
| Data Point | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Revenue Range | Dollar guidance (low-high) |
| Growth Rate | Nominal and constant currency |
| Segment Detail | Revenue by business unit |
| M&A Contribution | Acquisition impact |
