# Interpreting 8-K Analysis Results

## Reading the Output

The octagon-agent returns structured 8-K analysis with source citations:

**Filing Summary:**
- Filing date and form type
- Item categories disclosed
- Brief description of events

**Material Events:**
- Specific events disclosed
- Materiality assessment
- Related agreements or exhibits

## Assessing Event Materiality

### Quantitative Factors

| Factor | High Materiality | Low Materiality |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| Dollar Amount | >5% of assets/revenue | <1% of assets/revenue |
| Revenue Impact | Significant change | Marginal effect |
| Debt Level | Material obligation | Minor commitment |
| Equity Dilution | >5% of shares | <1% of shares |

### Qualitative Factors

| Factor | High Materiality | Low Materiality |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| Strategic Impact | Core business affected | Peripheral operations |
| Management Change | CEO, CFO, key officers | Lower-level positions |
| Customer Impact | Major customer affected | Minor relationships |
| Regulatory | Significant compliance | Routine matters |

## Analyzing Specific Event Types

### M&A Events (Item 2.01)

**Key Information to Extract:**
- Transaction value and structure
- Financing sources
- Expected closing date
- Integration plans
- Synergy expectations

**Red Flags:**
- Unusual terms or conditions
- Significant contingent payments
- Financing concerns
- Regulatory hurdles
- Material adverse change clauses

### Leadership Changes (Item 5.02)

**Key Information to Extract:**
- Who is departing/joining
- Effective date
- Reason (if disclosed)
- Succession plan
- Compensation arrangements

**Red Flags:**
- Sudden departure without successor
- Multiple executives leaving
- "Personal reasons" with no detail
- Departure during critical period
- No transition plan disclosed

### Financial Restatements (Item 4.02)

**Key Information to Extract:**
- Periods affected
- Accounts impacted
- Reason for non-reliance
- Expected restatement timeline
- Internal control implications

**Red Flags:**
- Revenue recognition issues
- Multiple periods affected
- Control weaknesses identified
- Auditor involvement
- SEC inquiry mentioned

### Material Agreements (Item 1.01)

**Key Information to Extract:**
- Counterparty
- Key terms and conditions
- Duration
- Financial commitments
- Termination provisions

**Red Flags:**
- Related party transactions
- Unusual terms
- Significant financial obligations
- Change of control provisions
- Non-compete restrictions

## Timing Analysis

### Filing Timing Signals

| Timing | Possible Interpretation |
|--------|------------------------|
| Friday after market | Minimize attention |
| Holiday period | Reduce scrutiny |
| Before market open | Allow trading reaction |
| After market close | Standard practice |
| Same day as event | Real-time disclosure |
| 4-day deadline | Routine compliance |

### Event Sequence

Track related filings:
1. **Initial 8-K** - Event announcement
2. **8-K/A** - Amendment with additional details
3. **10-Q/10-K** - Full integration of event
4. **DEF 14A** - Proxy details for votes

## Comparative Analysis

### Company History

Compare to prior 8-Ks:
- Similar events and outcomes
- Management response patterns
- Disclosure quality over time
- Frequency of certain items

### Peer Comparison

Compare to industry:
- Industry-standard disclosures
- Unique events
- Response timing
- Detail level

## Red Flags Summary

### Immediate Concerns

1. **Auditor resignation with disagreements**
2. **Restatement of multiple periods**
3. **Delisting notice received**
4. **Material weakness disclosed**
5. **Control change without premium**

### Watch Items

1. **CFO departure during reporting period**
2. **Multiple board resignations**
3. **Major customer loss**
4. **Significant impairment**
5. **Covenant violation**
