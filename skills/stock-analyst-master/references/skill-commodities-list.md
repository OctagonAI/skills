# Commodities List Skill

## Purpose

Retrieve catalog of tradable commodities across energy, metals, and agriculture.

## When to Use

- Researching commodity markets
- Understanding market participants
- Identifying trading opportunities
- Commodity exposure analysis

## Example Prompt

```
Retrieve the full catalog of tradable commodities across energy, metals, and agriculture.
```

## Key Information Returned

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Energy | Natural gas, LNG, power, oil, coal |
| Metals | Precious metals, base metals, ferrous |
| Agriculture | Grains, softs, livestock |
| Trading Companies | Market participants |
