# Skill: SEC Segment Reporting

## Purpose

Analyze business segment performance, margins, and geographic breakdown.

## When to Use

- Understanding business composition
- Segment-level profitability analysis
- Geographic exposure assessment
- Sum-of-parts valuation support

## Example Prompt

```
Analyze business segment performance and reporting from IBM's latest quarterly filing.
```

## Key Information Returned

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Segment Revenue | Revenue by business unit |
| Segment Margin | Profitability by segment |
| Geographic Mix | Revenue by region |
| Segment Trends | Growth rates |

## Integration with Other Skills

- **Pairs with**: sec-business-desc-analysis for context
- **Feeds into**: Business Overview section
- **Validates**: Segment performance claims

## Report Section Mapping

| Report Section | Usage |
|----------------|-------|
| Business Overview | Segment breakdown |
| Financial Analysis | Segment profitability |
| Risk Assessment | Concentration risk |
