# Earnings Market Expansion Skill

## Purpose
Identify geographic growth plans, new market launches, and product diversification.

## When to Use
- Understanding expansion plans
- New market entry tracking
- Geographic diversification

## Example Prompt
```
Identify market expansion and geographic growth plans discussed in UBER's latest earnings call.
```

## Key Information Returned
| Data Point | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| New Market Launches | Planned city/country launches |
| Product Diversification | New products/services |
| International Markets | Geographic targets |
| Growth Metrics | Expansion KPIs |
