# Interpreting Market Expansion Results

## Understanding Expansion Commentary

### Expansion Signal Strength

| Signal | Strength | Language Examples |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| Committed | High | "Launching in Q2," "investing $X" |
| Planned | Medium | "Planning to enter," "roadmap includes" |
| Exploring | Low | "Evaluating," "considering" |
| No Update | None | Not mentioned |

### Expansion Urgency Assessment

| Urgency | Timeline | Confidence |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Imminent | This quarter | Very high |
| Near-term | Next 2-3 quarters | High |
| Medium-term | 1-2 years | Medium |
| Long-term | 3+ years | Aspirational |

## Analyzing New Market Launches

### Launch Volume Analysis

## Financial Impact Analysis

### Expansion Contribution Model

```
Revenue Impact Calculation:

Current Revenue: $10B
- Core Markets (70%): $7.0B growing 10% = $7.7B
- Expansion Markets (30%): $3.0B growing 40% = $4.2B
Next Year Total: $11.9B (19% growth)

Contribution:
- Core: $0.7B (35% of growth)
- Expansion: $1.2B (65% of growth)

Key Insight: Majority of growth from expansion
```
