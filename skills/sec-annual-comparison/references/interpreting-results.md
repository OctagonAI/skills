# Interpreting Annual Comparison Results

## Reading the Output

The octagon-agent returns structured year-over-year comparison with source citations:

**Financial Metrics:**
- Current year vs. prior year values
- Percentage changes
- Key drivers of change

**Risk Factor Changes:**
- New, removed, or modified risks
- Priority shifts
- Emerging concerns

## Understanding Financial Comparisons

### Revenue Analysis

| Metric | How to Interpret |
|--------|------------------|
| Total Revenue | Overall business growth |
| Growth Rate | Momentum direction |
| Segment Mix | Business composition shift |
| Geographic Mix | Market expansion/contraction |

### Profitability Trends

| Trend | Positive | Negative |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Gross Margin | Expanding | Contracting |
| Operating Margin | Improving | Declining |
| Net Margin | Increasing | Decreasing |
| EPS | Growing | Falling |

### Balance Sheet Evolution

| Change | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Growing Assets | Expansion, investment |
| Rising Debt | Leverage, acquisitions |
| Cash Changes | Liquidity position |
| Equity Growth | Retained earnings, capital |

## Evaluating Change Significance

### Materiality Thresholds

| Change | Significance |
|--------|--------------|
| <5% | Immaterial, normal variance |
| 5-15% | Moderate, worth noting |
| 15-25% | Significant, investigate |
| >25% | Material, deep analysis needed |

### Context Matters

Consider changes relative to:
- Industry trends
- Peer performance
- Management guidance
- Economic conditions
- Company-specific events

## Risk Factor Evolution Analysis

|-------------|-------------|

## Multi-Year Trend Interpretation

### Trajectory Analysis

| Pattern | Assessment |
|---------|------------|

| Consistent decline | Structural issue |

### CAGR Calculation

For 3+ year trends:
- Revenue CAGR = (End/Start)^(1/Years) - 1
- Compare to industry, peers
- Assess sustainability

## Red Flags in Comparisons

### Financial Red Flags

1. **Revenue slowing** - Growth deceleration
2. **Margin compression** - Profitability pressure
3. **Cash declining** - Liquidity concerns
4. **Debt increasing** - Leverage risk
5. **Working capital deteriorating** - Operational stress

### Disclosure Red Flags

1. **Reduced transparency** - Less detail provided
2. **Changed metrics** - Different KPIs reported
3. **Segment reorganization** - May hide performance
4. **Accounting changes** - May mask trends
5. **Risk factor expansion** - Growing concerns
