## Red Flags

### Company Concerns

1. **Limited commodities** - Concentration risk
2. **Single geography** - Regional dependence
3. **Only financial** - No physical access
4. **No risk management** - Exposure issues

### Market Concerns

1. **Regulatory changes** - Impact on trading
2. **Supply disruption** - Geopolitical risk
3. **Demand shifts** - Technology changes
4. **Price volatility** - Market stress
