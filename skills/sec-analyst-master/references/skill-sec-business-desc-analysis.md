# Skill: SEC Business Description Analysis

## Purpose

Extract business descriptions and competitive landscape from 10-K Item 1.

## When to Use

- Understanding business model
- Competitive positioning analysis
- Identifying competitive advantages
- Mapping business segments

## Example Prompt

```
Extract and summarize the business description and competitive landscape from GOOGL's latest 10-K filing.
```

## Key Information Returned

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Business Structure | Segments and organization |
| Products/Services | Offerings description |
| Competition | Competitive landscape |
| Strategy | Strategic priorities |

## Integration with Other Skills

- **Pairs with**: sec-segment-reporting for segment details
- **Feeds into**: Business Overview section
- **Validates**: Marketing claims with required disclosures

## Report Section Mapping

| Report Section | Usage |
|----------------|-------|
| Business Overview | Primary source |
| Executive Summary | Company overview |
| Competitive Analysis | Peer comparison context |
