## Governance Red Flags

### Board Red Flags

1. **Low independence** - <50% independent
2. **Long tenure** - Average >12 years
3. **Overboarding** - Directors on 4+ public boards
4. **Low attendance** - <75% meeting attendance
5. **No diversity** - Homogeneous background

### Structural Red Flags

1. **Combined Chair/CEO, no lead** - Concentrated power
2. **Classified board** - Entrenchment
3. **No proxy access** - Limited shareholder voice
4. **Supermajority requirements** - High barriers
5. **Dual-class stock** - Unequal voting

### Compensation Red Flags

1. **No say-on-pay** - Limited accountability
2. **Failed say-on-pay** - <50% support
3. **No clawback** - Lack of protection
4. **Generous perks** - Excessive benefits
5. **Golden parachutes** - Large severance

### Related Party Red Flags

1. **Family employment** - Nepotism concern
2. **Large transactions** - Conflict of interest
3. **Charitable links** - Indirect benefits
4. **Recurring deals** - Dependency
5. **Vague disclosure** - Transparency issue
