# Skill: SEC Debt Covenant

## Purpose

Analyze debt covenants and credit agreement terms from SEC filings.

## When to Use

- Credit risk assessment
- Covenant compliance monitoring
- Debt maturity analysis
- Refinancing risk evaluation

## Example Prompt

```
Analyze debt covenants and credit agreement terms disclosed in F's latest SEC filings.
```

## Key Information Returned

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Debt Structure | Facilities and terms |
| Covenant Types | Financial and incurrence |
| Compliance Status | Current vs. required |
| Maturities | Debt repayment schedule |

## Integration with Other Skills

- **Pairs with**: sec-cash-flow-review for liquidity
- **Feeds into**: Liquidity & Capital section
- **Validates**: Debt risk exposure
